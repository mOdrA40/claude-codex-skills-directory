# Sharing, Data Products, and Collaboration

## Rules

- Sharing strategy should reflect domain ownership, trust, and lifecycle expectations.
- Data products need contracts, discoverability, and support posture.
- Collaboration should not undermine governance or cost visibility.
- Producer/consumer boundaries should be explicit.

## Practical Guidance

- Standardize how teams publish, document, and evolve shared datasets.
- Track who consumes critical shared assets.
- Align sharing with masking, retention, and SLA expectations.
- Avoid hidden dependencies on undocumented tables.

## Principal Review Lens

- Which shared dataset has the highest blast radius today?
- Are we enabling self-service or encouraging dependency chaos?
- What contract is missing between producers and consumers?
- Which shared asset most needs stronger product ownership?
