# Accessibility and Interaction Contracts

## Principle

Accessibility is not an afterthought layer. It is part of the component contract and must survive customization and product pressure.

## Focus Areas

- keyboard interaction
- focus management
- ARIA semantics where appropriate
- reduced motion and user preference handling
- error and validation clarity

## Review Questions

- what interaction contract does this component promise?
- what breaks when styling or composition changes?
- can teams accidentally ship inaccessible variants with this API?
