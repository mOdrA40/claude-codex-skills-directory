# Content Architecture and Collections in Astro

## Principle

Astro shines when content structure is explicit and operationally manageable. Weak content boundaries create editorial pain and brittle builds.

## Focus Areas

- content collections and schema discipline
- authoring workflows
- route generation and content taxonomy
- editorial ownership and review flow

## Review Questions

- how does content scale across locales, sections, and authors?
- what schema drift is most likely?
- what part of content publishing is hardest to debug today?
