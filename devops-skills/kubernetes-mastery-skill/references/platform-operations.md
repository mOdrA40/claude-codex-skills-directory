# Platform Operations

## Rules

- Cluster upgrades, node maintenance, and addon management should be staged and reversible.
- Platform ownership boundaries must be explicit.
- Operational maturity includes boring upgrades and consistent standards.
- Change management should reflect cluster criticality.

## Principal Review Lens

- What platform change carries the biggest hidden blast radius?
- Can the team upgrade safely during normal business cadence?
- Which addon or dependency is least owned today?
